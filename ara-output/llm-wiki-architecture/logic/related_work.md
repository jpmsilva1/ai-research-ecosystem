## RW01: Vannevar Bush, 1945
- **DOI**: Memex
- **Type**: baseline
- **Delta**: 
  - What changed: Bush envisioned a private, curated knowledge store with associative trails, but humans had to manually maintain it. The LLM-Wiki introduces the LLM as the autonomous maintainer.
  - Why: The maintenance burden caused humans to abandon personal wikis.
- **Claims affected**: C02
- **Adopted elements**: The concept of a private, curated knowledge store with associative trails.
